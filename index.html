More than food is an online platform created by Eva Massetti, who is shown in the picture below, with the aim of collecting testimonies of people recovered from eating disorders. After the Covid-19 pandemic, cases of eating disorders have grown exponentially. They can affect people of all genders, ages, races, social groups and religions.
